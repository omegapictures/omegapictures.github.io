Day 7 FAQ

What is Day 7?

Day 7 is a sci-fi/fantasy series that will introduce auidences to a new world while telling a single story from this world.  Unfolding over eight episodes, Day 7 ends where the next story begins, and will spin off into a nine part feature film franchise.

What work has been done on Day 7?

We crowdfunded and produced a proof of concept project that closely aligns with episodes 6 and 7 in the envisioned series.  We have a series bible and concept art, as well as scripts for the first --- episodes. We also have have scripts for seven of the nine feature films, and outlines for the other two.
